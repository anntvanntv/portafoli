My Work

A selection of my web projects

Website for the Inclusive Children's Story "Kleine Wesen und ein Ei".

The web provides information about the story, presents the characters in an artistic way and offers a wealth of additional information for parents and educators. For this website, in addition to the programming, I also created the music and the drawings.

Website with a shop for the record label Sello Fantasma.

The website offers information about the label's artists and an integrated online store with merchandise (T-shirts, albums).

Website "Gene Regulation of Cell Identity" Lab

Website for Mireya Plass, a scientist researcher and professor at the Univesity of Barcelona. The website featurs ongoing projects, scientific publications, members and news.

Website "Aúpa Che" Empanadas Berlín

Website for the empanadas stand "Aúpa Che" at Markhalle Neun in Berlin. The website provides information about the products and the stand and allows customers to request events or purchase a voucher.

Website for the professor Jo Seldeslachts

Website for Jo Seldeslachts, an economist in industrial organization and regulatory economics. It presents Jo Seldeslachts' current positions, research, and publications.

Website for the journalist Sivia Taulés

Website for Silvia Taulés, a journalist and writer based in Barcelona. The site features links to all her published articles—selected according to the media outlet in which they appeared—summaries of the books she has published through various publishers, a brief biography, and contact information.

Website Comic "Empezar por aquí

"Empezar por aquí" is a comic created by me, with original text and illustrations, published in a web format.
